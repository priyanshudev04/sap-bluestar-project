How FI, MM, and SD are connected in BlueStar's SAP landscape

Procure-to-Pay (P2P) When a Goods Receipt is posted (MIGO, Mvmt 101), SAP automatically creates FI document posting to Stock Account (BSX → GL 300000) and GR/IR Clearing (WRX → GL 191000).
T-Codes: ME21N → MIGO → MIRO
SD
Sales & Distrib.
→
FI
Financial Acctg
Order-to-Cash (O2C) Billing document (VF01) automatically generates FI document: Debit AR (GL 130000), Credit Revenue (GL 400000), Credit GST Payable (GL 220000). Customer payment clears the open item.
T-Codes: VA01 → VL01N → VF01 → F-28
MM
Materials Mgmt
→
SD
Sales & Distrib.
Inventory to Delivery When outbound delivery is goods-issued (VL02N), Movement Type 601 is triggered in MM — reducing stock from SL02 (Finished Goods). COGS is posted to GL 500000 via GBB account key in OBYC.
T-Codes: VL01N → VL02N (PGI) → MM stock reduced
SD
Sales & Distrib.
↔
FI
Financial Acctg
Credit Management During sales order creation (VA01), SAP checks the customer's credit exposure in FI (Credit Control Area BSCA). If the limit (configured in FD32) is exceeded, the order is blocked. Credit rep reviews via VKM1.
T-Codes: VA01 (credit check) → VKM1 (release) → FD32 (limits)
